Flowers Festival:

Upcoming Flowers Festival in Race Course Park!

We are proud to announce one of our most colorful and exciting events here in our beloved Lahore City from 31 January 2023 to 20 February 2023, from morning until 6 pm. We will celebrate our rich traditions with our yearly Flowers Festival. Come ready to be amazed by the variety of colors, designs and fragrances that will bring colors in your life. This activity will be a free event sponsored by our City Council members; come ready to enjoy a day at the park with friends and family and to make memories that will last for years to come.

Families, friends, and visitors from out of town gather year after year to spend an afternoon filled with entertainment and live music provided by some of our talented local musicians. We will also have vendors with delicious foods for purchase at low prices!

Don't miss this wonderful opportunity to strengthen bonds and friendship here in Lahore City.The weather will be fantastic to spend it outdoors, start spreading the news and save the date!
