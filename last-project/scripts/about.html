About Our Fruit Shop

We sell best organic fruits in the business that are directly coming from the farmers in the area, but whenever needed we use some natinoal and international suppliers.
We started exactly 10 years ago and since than our customer retention rate is 100% as customers are our top first priority. We never compromise on the quality. We started with the little juice corner a decade ago, but now we have 20 food trucks and we deliver your order max within 20 minutes.
We believe in eat fresh and stay healthy. We don't only provide best food, we also charge reasonable prices so everyone afford it and enjoy the fruits and juices. We motto is to make our people healthy and happy.
This has been an abosolute pleasure to serve you all over the years and we aim to continue our services in the years to come and make improvements whenever needed.

Our Service Area

Our food trucks cover the coastal area from Los Angeles to San Deigo.
Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visitng the coastal areas.

We deliver and manage orders over the phone and via the website in addition to take aways.
